Client Benefits

Clients that work with xAlt trainers have access to the worlds best trainers. xAlt trainers follow a training protocol to guarantee clients are receiving the proper training and reaching their goals.

More value

Clients receive guidance and coaching to confidently and successfully make progress on their goals.

Mobility screen

Designed by some of the best trainers in the industry, xAlt’s patented mobility screening finds and tracks the client's strengths and weaknesses, enabling xAlt trainers to dial in and design targeted programs and routines

exercise database

xAlt Trainers have access to the xAlt Exercise and Routine Database. Our trainers are able to submit and vote on the best exercises on the database. This gives xAlt trainers access to the best of best exercises to create the perfect routine for every client.

routine builder

xAlt's report builder utilizes the exercise database to create and track workout routines for each client. After completing a workout, both trainers and clients receive a report that logs the session details and progress made.

Built-in scheduling

With xAlt's scheduling software, client's will have access to their upcoming routines before their next session so they can set up ahead of time.
